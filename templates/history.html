History of our restaurant

Once upon a time, there lived a chef named Rishi in a small town. He brought his favorite spices from India and started cooking fro his friends. The food was so delicious that soon people from all over the area were coming to him.

Thus was born a small restaurant, which today is known as "Taste of India" Here they prepare curries, naan and aromaric masala tea, and every guest finds something special for themselves.

We believe that food brings people together and brings joy. That is hy our restaurant has become a metting place for many families and friends.
